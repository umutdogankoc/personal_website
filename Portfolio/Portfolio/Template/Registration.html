Contact Registration Form

Contact Registration Form is a ASP.NET Web Application, which provides a potential contact to fill up information properly. Once the form is submitted, there will be a new contact card created in NAV, and contact Questionnaire Profile will be fulfilled automatically. And relevant salespersons and sales manager will be notified via e-mail
